Spelunked Travel Agency

A site for spelunking tour information

Spelunked Travel Agency was a team project where we were tasked with creating a site for spelunking. For development our team used Java, Spring Framework, Thymeleaf, Hibernate, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

We also utilized some TDD, with JUnit, and GitHub as our repository, and Google Meets for our team meetings to discuss our Scrum sprints, which is the Agile tool we used for this project.
